The Problem:

The TARS website is significantly outdated and in desparate need of a facelift. The goal behind re-designing this website would:

  1. Increase accessibility
  2. Reduce roadblocks
  3. Fast track the fostering/adoption process

How might we unite and support people seeking companionship with animals in need of a home?

User Research

Trustworthiness and Reputation

  1. Users are less likely to adopt from donate to web-based adoption agencies that are not reputable or they perciee to be untrustworthy.
  2. 100% of those tested stated that they would be less likely to adopt from an outdated website,or a website that they determined as being poorly designed.

Access to Information

  1. Users value information. Potential adopters want access to as much information as possible, upfront, about their potential new pet.
  2. “Websites with limited information makes me nervous about the adoption process”.

Removing hurdles from adoption

  1. 50% of users stated they want the option of talking to a person if needed.
  2. 66% of users wanted the ability to apply for adoption online.
